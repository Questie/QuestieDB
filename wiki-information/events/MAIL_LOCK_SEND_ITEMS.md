## Event: MAIL_LOCK_SEND_ITEMS

**Title:** MAIL LOCK SEND ITEMS

**Content:**
Fired when you send an item that needs a confirmation (e.g. Heirlooms that are still refundable)
`MAIL_LOCK_SEND_ITEMS: attachSlot, itemLink`

**Payload:**
- `attachSlot`
  - *number* - Mail Slot
- `itemLink`
  - *string*
