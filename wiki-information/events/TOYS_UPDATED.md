## Event: TOYS_UPDATED

**Title:** TOYS UPDATED

**Content:**
Needs summary.
`TOYS_UPDATED: itemID, isNew, hasFanfare`

**Payload:**
- `itemID`
  - *number?* : ToyID
- `isNew`
  - *boolean?* - Whether the toy has just been added to your collection.
- `hasFanfare`
  - *boolean?* - Whether the toy should appear with a "fanfare" glow effect.
