## Event: DELETE_ITEM_CONFIRM

**Title:** DELETE ITEM CONFIRM

**Content:**
Fired when the player attempts to destroy an item.
`DELETE_ITEM_CONFIRM: itemName, qualityID, bonding, questWarn`

**Payload:**
- `itemName`
  - *string*
- `qualityID`
  - *number*
- `bonding`
  - *number*
- `questWarn`
  - *number*
