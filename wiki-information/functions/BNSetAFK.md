## Title: BNSetAFK

**Content:**
Sets the player's online AFK status.
`BNSetAFK(bool)`

**Parameters:**
- `bool`
  - *boolean* - true to set your battle.net status to AFK and false to unset it.

**Description:**
`BNSetDND(true)` can override AFK status.
`BNSetDND(false)` can't unset AFK status.
