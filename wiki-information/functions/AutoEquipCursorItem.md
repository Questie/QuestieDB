## Title: AutoEquipCursorItem

**Content:**
Equips the item currently held by the cursor.
`AutoEquipCursorItem()`

**Usage:**
```lua
PickupContainerItem(0,1)
AutoEquipCursorItem()
PickupContainerItem(0,1)
AutoEquipCursorItem()
```

**Miscellaneous:**
**Result:**
Equips the first item in your backpack to the relevant slot (if it can be equipped).
