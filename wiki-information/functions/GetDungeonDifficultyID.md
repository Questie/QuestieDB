## Title: GetDungeonDifficultyID

**Content:**
Returns the selected dungeon difficulty.
`difficultyID = GetDungeonDifficultyID()`

**Returns:**
- `difficultyID`
  - *number* : The player's (or group leader's) current dungeon difficulty ID preference.
