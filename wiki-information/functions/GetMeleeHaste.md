## Title: GetMeleeHaste

**Content:**
Returns the player's melee haste percentage.
`meleeHaste = GetMeleeHaste()`

**Returns:**
- `meleeHaste`
  - *number*

**Description:**
Related API:
- `GetCombatRating(CR_HASTE_MELEE)`
- `GetCombatRatingBonus(CR_HASTE_MELEE)`

**Usage:**
`/dump GetMeleeHaste()`
