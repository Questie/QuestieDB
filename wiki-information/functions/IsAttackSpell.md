## Title: IsAttackSpell

**Content:**
Returns true if a spellbook item is the "Auto Attack" spell.
`isAttack = IsAttackSpell(spellName)`

**Parameters:**
- `spellName`
  - *string* - The spell name to test.

**Returns:**
- `isAttack`
  - *Flag* - Returns 1 if the spell is the "Attack" spell, nil otherwise
