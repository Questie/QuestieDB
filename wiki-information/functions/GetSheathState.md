## Title: GetSheathState

**Content:**
Returns which type of weapon the player currently has unsheathed.
`sheathState = GetSheathState()`

**Returns:**
- `sheathState`
  - *number* - Currently unsheathed weapon type:
    - `Value`
    - `Weapon`
    - `1`
      - None
    - `2`
      - Melee
    - `3`
      - Ranged
