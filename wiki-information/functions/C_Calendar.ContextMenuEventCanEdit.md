## Title: C_Calendar.ContextMenuEventCanEdit

**Content:**
Returns whether the player can edit the event.
`canEdit = C_Calendar.ContextMenuEventCanEdit(offsetMonths, monthDay, eventIndex)`

**Parameters:**
- `offsetMonths`
  - *number*
- `monthDay`
  - *number*
- `eventIndex`
  - *number*

**Returns:**
- `canEdit`
  - *boolean*
