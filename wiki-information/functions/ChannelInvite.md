## Title: ChannelInvite

**Content:**
Invites the specified user to the channel.
`ChannelInvite(channelName, playerName)`

**Parameters:**
- `channelName`
  - *string* - The name of the channel to invite to
- `playerName`
  - *string* - The name of the player to invite

**Usage:**
`ChannelInvite("uimods", "Sembiance")`
