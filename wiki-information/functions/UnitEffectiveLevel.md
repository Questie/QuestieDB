## Title: UnitEffectiveLevel

**Content:**
Returns the unit's effective (scaled) level.
`level = UnitEffectiveLevel(unit)`

**Parameters:**
- `unit`
  - *string* : UnitId

**Returns:**
- `level`
  - *number*

**Description:**
When inebriated, the apparent level of hostile units is lowered by up to 5.
