## Title: C_KeyBindings.GetCustomBindingType

**Content:**
Returns the type of a custom binding.
`customBindingType = C_KeyBindings.GetCustomBindingType(bindingIndex)`

**Parameters:**
- `bindingIndex`
  - *number*

**Returns:**
- `customBindingType`
  - *Enum.CustomBindingType (nilable)*
    - `Value`
    - `Field`
    - `Description`
    - `0`
      - VoicePushToTalk
