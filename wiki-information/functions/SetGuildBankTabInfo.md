## Title: SetGuildBankTabInfo

**Content:**
Sets the name and icon of a guild bank tab.
`SetGuildBankTabInfo(tab, name, icon)`

**Parameters:**
- `tab`
  - *number* - Bank Tab to edit.
- `name`
  - *string* - New tab name.
- `icon`
  - *number* - FileID of the new icon texture.

**Reference:**
- `GetGuildBankTabInfo()`
