## Title: UnitSetRole

**Content:**
Sets a unit's role in the group.
`result = UnitSetRole(unit)`

**Parameters:**
- `unit`
  - *string* : UnitToken
- `roleStr`
  - *string?* : 

**Returns:**
- `result`
  - *boolean*

**Description:**
It's not possible to assign roles to classes that are not capable of fulfilling that role.
