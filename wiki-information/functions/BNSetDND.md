## Title: BNSetDND

**Content:**
Sets the player's online DND status.
`BNSetDND(bool)`

**Parameters:**
- `bool`
  - *boolean* - true to set your battle.net status to DND and false to unset it.

**Description:**
`BNSetAFK(true)` can override DND status.
`BNSetAFK(false)` can't unset DND status.
