## Title: ChannelBan

**Content:**
Bans a player from the specified channel.
`ChannelBan(channelName, playerName)`

**Parameters:**
- `channelName`
  - *string* - The name of the channel to ban on
- `playerName`
  - *string* - The name of the player to ban

**Usage:**
`ChannelBan("uimods", "Sembiance")`
