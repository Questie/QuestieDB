## Title: C_QuestLog.GetQuestWatchType

**Content:**
Returns the watchType associated with a given quest.
`watchType = C_QuestLog.GetQuestWatchType(questID)`

**Parameters:**
- `questID`
  - *number*

**Returns:**
- `watchType`
  - *Enum.QuestWatchType?*
    - `Value`
    - `Field`
    - `Description`
    - `0`
      - Automatic
    - `1`
      - Manual
