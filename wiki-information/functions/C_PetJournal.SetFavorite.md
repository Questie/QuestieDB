## Title: C_PetJournal.SetFavorite

**Content:**
Sets (or clears) the pet as a favorite.
`C_PetJournal.SetFavorite(petID, value)`

**Parameters:**
- `petID`
  - *string* - Unique identifier for this specific pet
- `value`
  - *number* -
    - `0`: Pet is not a favorite
    - `1`: Pet is a favorite
