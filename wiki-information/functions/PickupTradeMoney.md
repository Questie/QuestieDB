## Title: PickupTradeMoney

**Content:**
Places an amount of money from the player's trade offer onto the cursor.
`PickupTradeMoney(copper)`

**Parameters:**
- `copper`
  - *number* - amount of money, in copper, to pick up.

**Reference:**
- `PickupPlayerMoney`
- `AddTradeMoney`
- `SetTradeMoney`
