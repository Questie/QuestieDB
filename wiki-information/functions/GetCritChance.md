## Title: GetCritChance

**Content:**
Returns the melee critical hit chance percentage.
`critChance = GetCritChance()`

**Returns:**
- `critChance`
  - *number* - The player's melee critical hit chance, as a percentage; e.g. 5.3783211 corresponding to a ~5.38% crit chance.
